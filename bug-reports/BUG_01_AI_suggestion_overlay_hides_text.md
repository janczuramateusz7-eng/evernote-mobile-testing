# BUG 01 — AI suggestion overlay hides content in search results

## Description
Text content becomes partially hidden when selecting AI suggestion in the search feature.

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the Evernote app
2. Tap on the search bar
3. Tap “Wsparcie AI”
4. Tap any suggested prompt
5. When the “Czy chodzi Ci o…” suggestion appears, tap on it
6. Observe the result

---

## Expected Result
Text content should be fully visible and not covered by any overlay.

---

## Actual Result
Text content is partially hidden by a newly opened overlay, making it difficult to read.

---

## Severity
Major

## Priority
Medium

---

## Environment
- App Version: Evernote 10.17.1.1 (1245306)
- Device: Realme RMX3085
- Platform: Android
- OS Version: Android 13

---

## Notes
The issue impacts usability and readability of search results.
